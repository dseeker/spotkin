Frequently Asked Questions

How long does beta testing take?

Just 15-30 minutes of your time. You'll test the core monitoring features and share quick feedback about your experience.

Do I need to install anything?

Nope! That's the magic of SpotKin. It works directly in your browser - no downloads, no app store visits, no account creation required.

What devices can I test on?

Any device with a camera and modern browser - phones, tablets, laptops, desktops. We especially want feedback from the device you'd actually use for monitoring.

When will beta testing start?

Immediately after you're accepted! You'll get a beta access link within 24 hours of applying and can test at your convenience.

Will my data be stored or shared?

Absolutely not. SpotKin processes everything locally on your device. No video data is transmitted or stored anywhere. Your privacy is our priority.
