1. What This Policy Covers

This Cookie Policy explains how SpotKin ("we," "our," or "us") uses cookies, local storage, and similar technologies when you use our AI monitoring service.

Our Approach: SpotKin takes a minimal approach to cookies and data storage. We use only essential technologies needed for the service to function properly and securely.

Privacy-First: SpotKin does not use advertising cookies, tracking pixels, or third-party marketing cookies. We prioritize your privacy over data collection.

2. What Are Cookies and Similar Technologies?

2.1 Cookies

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device when you visit a website. They help websites remember information about your visit and preferences.

2.2 Local Storage

Local Storage is a web technology that allows websites to store data directly in your browser. Unlike cookies, this data is not automatically sent to servers and remains on your device.

2.3 Session Storage

Session Storage temporarily stores data for the duration of your browser session. This data is automatically deleted when you close your browser tab.

2.4 IndexedDB

IndexedDB is a browser database system that allows us to store larger amounts of structured data locally on your device for offline functionality.

3.2 Security and Encryption

  • AES-256-GCM Encryption: All sensitive data stored locally is encrypted using military-grade encryption
  • Secure Key Management: Encryption keys are generated and stored securely on your device
  • Data Integrity: Stored data includes integrity checks to prevent tampering
  • Automatic Cleanup: Old and unused data is automatically cleaned up to conserve space

3.3 Progressive Web App (PWA) Storage

SpotKin is a Progressive Web App (PWA) that uses additional storage for enhanced functionality:

  • Service Worker Cache: Stores application files for offline access
  • Application Cache: Caches critical resources for faster loading
  • Background Sync Queue: Temporary storage for data awaiting synchronization
  • Push Notification Data: Stores notification preferences and queue

7. Your Rights and Choices

7.1 GDPR Rights (EU Residents)

Under GDPR, you have specific rights regarding cookies and data storage:

  • Consent: Right to give or withdraw consent for non-essential cookies
  • Access: Right to know what data is stored and how it's used
  • Deletion: Right to have your stored data deleted
  • Portability: Right to export your data in a readable format
  • Objection: Right to object to data processing for analytics

7.2 How to Exercise Your Rights

  • In-App Controls: Use SpotKin's settings to export or delete your data immediately
  • Browser Controls: Use your browser's privacy settings to manage cookies and storage
  • Contact Us: Email us for assistance with data requests
  • Supervisory Authority: You can file complaints with your local data protection authority
