Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2025

Effective Date: January 2025

1. Introduction

SpotKin ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use our AI-powered monitoring service.

Our Privacy-First Approach: SpotKin is designed with privacy as a core principle. Most processing happens directly on your device, minimizing data transmission and maximizing your control over your information.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Camera and Visual Data

  • Camera Access: We access your device's camera only when you explicitly grant permission and activate monitoring
  • Image Processing: Images are sent to secure AI services (Puter.js) for analysis when monitoring is active
  • No Image Storage: We do not store, upload, or retain camera images or video footage
  • Analysis Results: Only text-based analysis results (e.g., "baby sleeping peacefully") are temporarily stored locally

2.2 Application Data

  • User Preferences: Monitoring settings, alert preferences, and application configurations
  • Monitoring History: Text-based analysis results and timestamps (stored locally with encryption)
  • Usage Statistics: Basic application usage metrics for performance optimization (anonymized)

2.3 Technical Information

  • Device Information: Browser type, device type, screen resolution (for optimal user experience)
  • Performance Data: Application performance metrics and error logs (anonymized)
  • Network Information: IP address (only for basic service functionality, not stored)

3. How We Use Your Information

3.1 Service Provision

  • Process camera images for AI-powered monitoring and analysis
  • Generate alerts and notifications based on detected activities
  • Maintain monitoring history and preferences for your convenience
  • Provide voice alerts and text-to-speech functionality

3.2 Service Improvement

  • Analyze usage patterns to improve application performance (anonymized data only)
  • Debug and fix technical issues to enhance user experience
  • Develop new features based on user needs and feedback

3.3 Security and Compliance

  • Protect against unauthorized access and security threats
  • Comply with legal obligations and regulatory requirements
  • Maintain system integrity and prevent abuse

4. Data Storage and Security

4.1 Local Storage

SpotKin uses a "privacy-by-design" approach where most data is stored locally on your device:

  • User Preferences: Stored in your browser's local storage with AES-256-GCM encryption
  • Monitoring History: Encrypted and stored locally, under your complete control
  • No Cloud Storage: Camera images and personal data are never uploaded to our servers

4.2 Security Measures

  • Military-Grade Encryption: AES-256-GCM encryption for all sensitive data
  • Secure Key Management: Encryption keys generated and stored securely on your device
  • Input Validation: Comprehensive protection against XSS and injection attacks
  • Content Security Policy: Strict security headers preventing unauthorized access
  • HTTPS Only: All communications use secure, encrypted connections

4.3 Data Retention

  • Monitoring History: Retained locally until you choose to delete it
  • User Preferences: Stored until you clear your browser data or uninstall
  • Analytics Data: Aggregated, anonymized data retained for up to 2 years for service improvement

5. Data Sharing and Third Parties

5.1 No Sale of Personal Data

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties. Your privacy is not a commodity.

5.2 AI Processing Services

SpotKin uses third-party AI services for image analysis. These services receive only:

  • Image Data: Individual camera frames for analysis (no personal identification)
  • Context Prompts: General monitoring instructions (e.g., "analyze for pet behavior")
  • No Personal Information: No names, locations, or identifying information is shared
  • No Data Retention: AI services are instructed not to store or learn from your data

AI Service Partners: We use Puter.js and Pollinations.ai for AI processing. These services have their own privacy policies, which we encourage you to review.

5.3 Service Providers

We may share limited, non-personal information with trusted service providers for:

  • Web hosting and content delivery
  • Analytics and performance monitoring (anonymized data only)
  • Security monitoring and threat detection

5.4 Legal Requirements

We may disclose information if required by law, regulation, or legal process, but only to the minimum extent necessary.

6. Your Privacy Rights

6.1 GDPR Rights (EU Residents)

Under GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Access: Request information about the personal data we hold about you
  • Rectification: Correct inaccurate or incomplete personal data
  • Erasure: Request deletion of your personal data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Portability: Export your data in a machine-readable format
  • Objection: Object to processing of your personal data
  • Restriction: Request restricted processing of your personal data

6.2 How to Exercise Your Rights

  • Data Export: Use the "Export Data" feature in the application settings
  • Data Deletion: Use the "Clear All Data" option in settings, or clear your browser data
  • Access Requests: Contact us using the information provided below
  • Complaints: You have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority

6.3 Control Your Data

Complete Control: Since most data is stored locally on your device, you have immediate control over your information. You can delete all data instantly through your browser settings or our application controls.

7. Cookies and Tracking

7.1 Essential Cookies

SpotKin uses minimal, essential cookies and local storage for:

  • Storing your application preferences and settings
  • Maintaining session state and security
  • Ensuring proper application functionality

7.2 Analytics (Optional)

We may use privacy-compliant analytics to understand how users interact with our service. This data is:

  • Completely anonymized and aggregated
  • Cannot be linked back to individual users
  • Used only for improving service performance
  • Can be opted out of in application settings

7.3 No Advertising Trackers

SpotKin does not use advertising cookies or third-party tracking pixels. We do not participate in advertising networks or behavioral tracking.

8. Children's Privacy

SpotKin is designed for use by adults (18+) to monitor children and pets. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The service processes images of children only as part of the monitoring functionality requested by parents or guardians.

Parental Control: Parents and guardians have complete control over all monitoring data, as it is stored locally on their devices.

9. International Data Transfers

SpotKin's privacy-first design minimizes international data transfers:

  • Local Processing: Most data processing occurs on your device, regardless of location
  • AI Services: When using AI analysis, data may be processed by services located in various countries
  • No Permanent Storage: No personal data is permanently stored outside your device
  • Adequate Protection: All data transfers use encryption and follow privacy standards
