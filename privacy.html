Privacy Policy

Effective Date: August 16, 2025

Last Updated: August 16, 2025

Our Privacy Commitment

SpotKin is designed with privacy as a fundamental principle. We believe that your monitoring data, preferences, and usage patterns should remain private and under your control. This privacy policy explains how we handle your information in our AI-powered monitoring application.

Key Privacy Benefits

  • All AI processing happens locally on your device
  • No camera feeds or images are transmitted to our servers
  • Your data is encrypted and stored securely
  • You can export or delete all your data at any time

Information We Collect

Data Stored Locally

The following information is stored locally on your device using encrypted storage:

  • User Preferences: Your monitoring settings, sensitivity levels, and alert preferences
  • Monitoring History: Timestamps, AI analysis results, and alert records
  • Zone Configurations: Custom monitoring zones you create
  • Usage Statistics: App performance metrics and feature usage

Data We Do NOT Collect

  • Camera feeds or video streams
  • Individual images or photographs
  • Personal identification information
  • Location data or device identifiers
  • Contact information or communications

Local AI Processing

SpotKin uses artificial intelligence to analyze your camera feed, but this analysis happens entirely on your device:

  • Local Processing: All AI analysis is performed locally using the Puter.js AI engine
  • No Image Transmission: Camera images never leave your device
  • Offline Capability: The app functions fully offline with cached AI models
  • Privacy by Design: Technical architecture ensures data cannot be extracted

Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data:

  • AES-256 Encryption: All sensitive data is encrypted using military-grade encryption
  • Secure Storage: Data is stored in encrypted browser storage with integrity checks
  • Content Security Policy: Strict security headers prevent unauthorized access
  • Input Validation: All user inputs are validated and sanitized
  • Regular Security Audits: We conduct regular security assessments

External Services

SpotKin integrates with minimal external services:

Puter.js AI Service

  • Purpose: Provides AI analysis capabilities when online
  • Data Sent: Only processed analysis requests, no raw images
  • Fallback: Local mock AI when service is unavailable
  • Privacy Policy: Puter Privacy Policy

Content Delivery Networks (CDNs)

  • Tailwind CSS: Styling framework loaded from CDN
  • Font Awesome: Icon library loaded from CDN
  • Data Collection: These services may collect standard web analytics

Your Rights (GDPR Compliance)

You have complete control over your data with the following rights:

  • Right to Access: Export all your data in JSON format
  • Right to Deletion: Permanently delete all stored data
  • Right to Portability: Download your data for use elsewhere
  • Right to Rectification: Modify or correct your preferences
  • Right to Restriction: Control data processing through app settings

Exercise Your Rights

Access these features through the app settings:

  • Settings → Privacy → Export Data
  • Settings → Privacy → Delete All Data
  • Settings → Preferences → Manage Settings

Analytics and Monitoring

We use privacy-compliant analytics to improve the app:

  • Performance Metrics: App load times, error rates, and usage patterns
  • Feature Usage: Which features are used most to guide development
  • Anonymization: All analytics data is aggregated and anonymized
  • Opt-Out: Analytics can be disabled in app settings

Children's Privacy

SpotKin is designed to monitor children but does not collect children's personal information:

  • No personal information is collected from users of any age
  • Camera feeds are processed locally and never transmitted
  • Parents retain full control over all monitoring data
  • The app complies with COPPA (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act)

Progressive Web App (PWA) Considerations

As a PWA, SpotKin has additional privacy characteristics:

  • Local Installation: App can be installed and run offline
  • Service Workers: Enable offline functionality while respecting privacy
  • Background Sync: Queues data locally until connectivity is restored
  • Push Notifications: Alerts are generated locally, not from servers

Legal Basis for Processing

Our legal basis for processing data under GDPR:

  • Legitimate Interest: Providing monitoring functionality as requested
  • Consent: User consent for optional features like analytics
  • Performance of Service: Essential app functionality

Policy Updates

We may update this privacy policy to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements:

  • Material changes will be prominently displayed in the app
  • You will be notified before changes take effect
  • Continued use constitutes acceptance of updated terms
  • Previous versions are available upon request

Contact Information

For questions about this privacy policy or data practices:

  • In-App Support: Settings → Help → Privacy Questions
  • Data Controller: SpotKin Development Team
  • Response Time: We aim to respond within 72 hours
  • Regulatory Authority: You may contact your local data protection authority

This privacy policy is effective as of August 16, 2025 and applies to all users of SpotKin.
